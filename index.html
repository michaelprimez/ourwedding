Welcome to Our Wedding App page.

Our Wedding is an application for iOS and Android that allow couples to present to their guests all the needed informations about their wedding

Who it works

Couples distribute the invitations to their guests referencing the app. The invitation also contains the credentials to access the content of the app. Users/Guests register to the wedding and to the pre-wedding events.

Description

Our Wedding app is a complementary to our invitations. It contains information about:

  • The schedule of pre-wedding event.
  • The schedule of the wedding day.
  • Place that must visit.
  • Accommodations and special offers about it.
  • All the available transportation that can use in the island.

Are hope is, our guests to have all the valiable information and enjoy with us our important day.
